Thursday Night Events, Cranked Up Irie Style

Thursday Night Events, Cranked Up Irie Style

WHEN?

Every Thursday from 8 p.m. to 12 a.m.
at Ting Irie Downtown Dubai.

Live music and banging Afro-Caribbean tunes aren’t the only tings that make our Skankin’ Thursdays THE go-to mid-week party. It’s the whole vibe we put out into the Dubai nightlife scene: warm, infectiously upbeat and all in all, a good time.

On our menu, you’ll find Jamaican kitchen staples with a fusion twist, like the oxtail sandwiches and mini Oman burgers. And to keep the dial a firm 11/10, our bar will be slinging some of the tastiest cocktails and mocktails in town.

Downtown Dubai’s Hottest Party Spot

When you’re in Dubai and looking for a place to let loose, our Ting Irie DXB location is where it’s at. Feel the Kingston energy in one of Dubai’s busiest districts as soon as you step through our doors.
